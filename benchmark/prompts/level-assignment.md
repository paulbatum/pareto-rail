## Benchmark additions

Aim for a **60-second playable run**. A duration from **55 to 65 seconds** is acceptable when needed to end on a natural musical phrase. This covers active gameplay after START and before the run summary; attract mode and REPLAY are outside it.

The expected standard is a polished showcase level, not merely a gate-passing implementation. After reaching mechanical completeness, use the remaining session to inspect and refine the player-facing result rather than stopping at the first passing build.

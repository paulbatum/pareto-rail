# Mass Driver

You are the payload chambered in an orbital railgun: a sixty-second ride from cold breech to open space, with one accelerator ring crossed on every beat. The bore physically stretches as the gun accelerates, its electrical heat climbs from arc blue through violet to white, and the six safety interlocks arrive on a hard deadline before the bar-28 shot.

## Visual language
Near-black vacuum steel, thin gunmetal facets, arc-blue conductor rails, violet charge, and small ion-white cores keep the bore legible without bloom. One hundred thirteen accelerator rings are laid directly onto the integrated speed curve; downbeats carry heavy diagonal coil housings. Coils clamp to the wall, threaders braid double helices through the center, capacitor staves break away around exposed cores, unstable arc bolts reconfigure every frame, and six hazard-amber X-clamps brood at the frame rim. The successful shot tears the fog and barrel away into stars and a distant white beacon.

## Musical language
128 BPM minimal techno in E minor, exactly 32 bars. A quiet tuned accelerator strike marks every pre-shot quarter note, binding all 112 playable ring crossings to the audible distance grid. The arrangement grows from sparse injection pulses into four-on-floor drive, sixteenth-note stage-2 pressure and a resonant acid line, then Em–F Phrygian klaxons and a full final-bar roll. A detuned saw-and-sine rail hum climbs every bar and stops at the shot; success lands an E-major bloom and sparse glass delays. Locks, volleys, chips, kills, armor breaks, interlock confirmations, rejects, and hull hits are transport-quantized and pitched from the live harmony, with section timbres crossfaded through each transition.

## Mechanical signature
A three-point hull and five hostile kinds support an authored rise in pressure: wall-orbiting coil ranks, counter-rotating threader weaves, four-hit two-stage capacitors, interceptable homing arc lightning, and six three-hit interlocks that station-keep around the bore. Clear every clamp before bar 28 to fire and preserve access to S rank; leave one standing and the charge detonates the barrel. Perfect six-target volleys earn a large clean-clear bonus.

# Deluge

A rain-lashed neon megacity escape with a hunter-gunship on the rail: freefall, streetfall, undercity, tube, canal, Vulture fight, and climb-out sections.

## Visual language
Cyan, magenta, slate, amber, green, and red city lighting; rain, lightning, panels, avenues, windows, tunnels, canals, traffic, train moments, and Vulture hardpoints.

## Musical language
176 BPM with rain ambience, D minor-centered chord material, boss chords, lightning and train synchronization, and player action sounds routed through the level mix.

## Mechanical signature
A 150-second run with a 5-point hull, gnats, interceptors, turrets, barriers, dropvans, bolts, flak, and a Vulture boss with pods and core phases.

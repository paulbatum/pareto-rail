# Rush

Rush is a near-black boost tunnel for testing how fast this engine can feel. Rectangular ribs, dashed rail strips, camera banking, FOV pulls, streak particles, and radial blur all push toward an almost uncomfortable sense of forward velocity.

## Visual language
Hard-edged cyan and amber geometry over black fog: close tunnel ribs, hot strobe gates, thin speed streaks, dashed guide rails, compact target silhouettes, and square procedural START/REPLAY glyphs that remain readable without bloom.

## Musical language
170 BPM and deliberately blunt: a relentless kick, closed hat, and one distorted bass figure. Player actions answer with terse zaps, ticks, and rejects inside the same harsh electronic palette.

## Mechanical signature
A 30-second speed-feel testbed with a long rail, authored speed surges, simple pods, strafing darts, and heavier armored targets. Fairness is secondary to passage frequency and near-field parallax.

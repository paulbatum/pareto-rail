# Mass Driver

Build a level where you're riding a payload down a huge orbital railgun. The whole thing is basically a tunnel of glowing accelerator rings and you pass through one ring exactly on every beat, so the speed and the music are the same thing. As the run goes on the rings get spaced further apart (you're accelerating) but you still hit them on the beat, and they glow hotter as you go faster. Keep it minimal: thin bright lines on black, no big glowing surfaces.

Enemies are debris and defense drones threading between the coils. Use the rings to frame targets.

Boss: the gun's safety mechanisms are jammed and the final firing charge is already building. You can hear it - rising hum, rings burning hotter, the whole tunnel ramping up. Blow up the jammed safety interlocks before the charge peaks. Clear them all in time and the gun fires, launching you out of the muzzle into open space at insane speed, everything goes quiet, level ends. Too slow and the charge has nowhere to go - the barrel blows with you in it.
